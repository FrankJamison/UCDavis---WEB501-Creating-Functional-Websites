About Frank Jamison

Thank you for taking the time to view my online resume. As you know, my name is Frank Jamison, and I am a web developer and computer programmer living in Hemet, California.

My educational background includes associate degrees in Math/Science, Humanities, Liberal Arts Studies, And Social/Behavioral Science from Mt. San Jacinto College in San Jacinto, California; a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from National University in La Jolla, CA; and I am currently working toward a master's degree in Information Technology with a concentration in Web Design at Southern New Hampshire University in Manchester, New Hampshire; and toward a Web Development Certificate at UC Davis in Davis, California.

In addition to formal degrees, I hold several certificates from various online learning portals such as Allison.com, Lynda.com, and Pluralsight.com, ranging in topics from Accounting and Microsoft Office Software to Front-End Web Development and Search Engine Optimization.

I enjoy membership in such national organizations as The American Association for the Advancement of Science, the National Society of Leadership and Success, and the Society for Technical Communication. I have also been previously affiliated with both the Association for Computing Machinery and the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers.

I am currently employed as a help desk associate in the technical support department for Broadridge Advisor Solutions in San Diego, CA where I assist financial advisors across the nation from companies such as Wells Fargo Advisors, Waddell & Reed, and New York Life to create, update, and maintain their online web presence.

For a more in-depth review of my employment history and qualifications, please have a look around my website. If you like what you see, feel free to contact me about any employment opportunities you feel I am a match for. I look forward to hearing from you.
